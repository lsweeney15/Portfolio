Who I am

Liam Sweeney was born in New Haven, Connecticut to a family that – wonderful as they may be – couldn’t draw a straight line with a ruler. Shocked by Liam’s early drawings, his parents first made sure there hadn’t been a switch at the hospital, and then encouraged him to develop and explore his artistic inclinations. He’s been doing so ever since.

Liam is a graphic designer with a focus on branding, web design, and product/packaging design. In addition to being well versed in Adobe Creative Suite, Liam is a front-end developer with fluency in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript and jQuery.

Liam's recent clients include: Bulltoro Watches, Upset Gentlemen, The Miami City Ballet, Teach For America Miami-Dade, ESPN, Dress For Success Miami, Robert K. Futterman & Associates, Terranova Corporation, Marriott, Hilton, and Disney's The Lion King Website.

watchIndex

App Development

watchIndex is an application for those who are simply overwhelmed by all of the great movies and television shows that are available at the click of a button. But where do you find them? What streaming service? What channel? What provider? With the click of a button, watchIndex allows the consumer to search their desired television show or movie, and where they can watch it. watchIndex also provides suggested content, which is updated daily by the MovieDB API. Future development plans: watchIndex’s future development includes a handful of click events to make navigating the site easier. User login/registration information will also provide an opportunity for advertising suggested content directly to the consumer’s email address.

Froogle

App Development

Too much time is spent searching for the best offers on an item across a multitude of different vendors. Froogle eliminates that for the consumer.

Froogle is an application for those looking for the best deal on an item but do not have the time to scour the internet…so, everyone.

Froogle uses API requests from two major retailers, Amazon and Best Buy, to compare their best prices on the searched item. In one click, you are provided with the best price for your searched item.

    FUTURE DEVELOPMENT PLANS:
  • Froogle’s future development includes an updated UX/UI.
  • Expansion of the search database to include API’s from retailers such as Ebay and Walmart
